Teams and Drivers

Who drives for who?

A detailed explanation!

In the current Formula One season, the grid is diverse and competitive, featuring a range of top teams and talented drivers. Mercedes-AMG Petronas has Lewis Hamilton, a seven-time World Champion known for his remarkable skill and consistency, alongside George Russell, a rising star with a knack for delivering strong performances. Red Bull Racing fields Max Verstappen, the 2021 World Champion recognized for his aggressive driving, and Sergio Pérez, a seasoned driver with a wealth of experience.

Scuderia Ferrari's lineup includes Charles Leclerc, a young and highly skilled driver making waves in the sport, and Carlos Sainz Jr., known for his versatility and speed. McLaren boasts Lando Norris, a promising young talent, and Oscar Piastri, a rookie with significant potential. Alpine features Esteban Ocon, a determined and skilled driver, and Pierre Gasly, known for his dynamic driving style.

Alfa Romeo Racing has Valtteri Bottas, a former Mercedes driver with a strong track record, and Zhou Guanyu, a talented rookie from China. Haas F1 Team includes Kevin Magnussen, known for his aggressive racing, and Nico Hülkenberg, a veteran driver returning to the sport. Finally, Williams Racing fields Alex Albon, a driver with a proven ability to perform under pressure, and Logan Sargeant, an exciting newcomer.

Together, these teams and drivers create an exhilarating and competitive season, each bringing their own strengths to the thrilling world of Formula One.
